welcome to zeemas

Zeemass is specialized in making customized team kits, uniforms jersey legging for equestrian and base layer etc.

Our service extended from producing custom-made cricket, soccer, hockey equestrian and other sports clothing for amateur teams to developing a complete range of merchandise for professional organizations completed with embroidered logo and printed name & numbers etc

If you want us to manufacture any special item from Sialkot or customize any item with your own logos, brand name or size charts etc., please contact us without any hesitation as we are ready every time for your service.

Who we are

Zeemass is trusted worldwide supplier and exporter for sports equipment, sports wears, Casual wear Soccer Uniforms and Fitness wear, Equestrian wear equipment and other sports equipment.

Zeemass management is Skilled and professional, having a vast experience in planning, manufacturing, quality control, quality assurance, distribution, export industry and having excellent reputation amongst the business community of the Pakistan being a well-known name due to experience and high level of skills in Sportswear business.
